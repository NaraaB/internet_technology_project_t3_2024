Poděbrady

Geographic Location:

Podebrady is a historic spa town located in the Central Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic, situated on the banks of the Elbe River (Labe). It lies approximately 50 kilometers east of Prague.

Population:

As of 2021, the town has a population of around 14,000 residents.

Climate:

podébrady experiences a temperate continental climate with warm summers and cold winters, making it ideal for year-round visits.
